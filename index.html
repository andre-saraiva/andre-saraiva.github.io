Short Bio

PhD student at the Institute of Computing at Universidade Federal Fluminense (UFF) since 2017. I have a master's degree in Computer Systems at Universidade Federal Fluminense (UFF) Brazil, since 2017, postgraduate in Architecture and Cloud Computing Project at Universidade Estácio de Sá (UNESA ) since 2020 and graduated in Computer Systems Technology at Universidade Federal Fluminense (UFF) Brazil, since 2015. I develop research in the area of Wireless Sensor Networks, with a special focus on energy efficiency and routing. Distance mediator of the Computer Systems Technology Course at Universidade Federal Fluminense through the UAB / CECIERJ / CEDERJ consortium of the disciplines of Linear Algebra and Programming with Graphical Interfaces. Participant in the MídiaCom Research Laboratory for Multimedia Data Communication, at the Computing Institute of the Universidade Federal Fluminense (UFF) and in 2021 I entered the Pedagogical Training course in Mathematics - Licenciatura at the Universidade Norte do Paraná (UNOPAR).
